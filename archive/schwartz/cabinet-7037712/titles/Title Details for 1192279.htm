Tape was azimuth adjusted on an Otari MTR-12 using the full track head, output in cal in A1.8 with signal routed through Maselec MTC-2 master control with +8 dB gain to Prism ADA-8XR analog to digital converters to Pyramix DAW version 7.0.11 SP3 Build 12120 21.04.2011. A: Conductor, word of mouth, radio, magazine and paper reviews, salesman recommendation. Q: When do you listen to records? A: when it's quiet, in the vening, when ironing, after work. Q: When do you buy records? A: In Winter, when I need to hear something in particular, on a binge. (short break ). Q: What type of records do you buy? A: Classical and Folk. Q: How do you feel about stereo? A: Like it. (short break) 3:32, Talk of clarity of old vs. new records.
