Engineer notes (Brad) for RXF 7326: Technical notes: Full track mono acetate tape with splices on 7" reel. The reel was recorded at 7 1/2 ips unitl 11:45 where it switches to 15 ips and then back to 7 1/2 ips at 15:15 of the tape. Tape was azimuth adjusted on Otari MTR-12 in A1.8 with signal routed through Maselec MTC-2 master control with +4 dB of gain to Prism ADA-8XR analog to digital converters to Pyramix DAW version 7.0.11 SP3 Build 12120 21.04.2011.
