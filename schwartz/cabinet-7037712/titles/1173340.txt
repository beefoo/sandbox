Opera and the baby

Summary

RXF 3680:
Opera and the baby (Tony Schwartz, narrator ; Reenah Schwartz, speaker and singer ; unidentified man, singer ; unidentified orchestra) (0:00).

Depicts the relationship between the conscious and the subconscious through the story of Reenah (named Micaela after a character in the opera Carmen). Many years after composing a lullaby, she discovers the song was influenced by the musical passage in the opera for Don Jose's entrance.
